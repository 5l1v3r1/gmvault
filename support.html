Support the Gmvault project

Thanks a lot for the warm support I have received since the launch of Gmvault.
The success of Gmvault has been overwhelming as since its debut on the 7th of May 2012 it has been downloaded more than 50 000 times.
If you would like to do more for the Gmvault project, have a look below at the different ways to help Gmvault getting an essential tool for all Gmailers.

Donate to the Gmvault project


Why donating to Gmvault ?


Below are some reasons why you should consider helping the Gmvault project:

  • You use happily Gmvault and think that it is a very helpful software and would like it to reach an even higher quality level,
  • You think that Gmvault is a necessary tool filling a gap in the Gmail service as Gmail will not allow you to recover all your emails in case of issues,
  • You would like to see new features in future versions such as for example a graphical interface, facilities for helping Gmail for Business admins, automatic cloud savings ...,
  • You want to show your interest in having Gmvault maintained and supported professionally,
  • You want to thank the developer for the time and efforts dedicated to Gmvault,
  • You believe in open source projects and want Gmvault to stay open source,
  • You have your own motives and want to sponsor Gmvault,
  • You are in a good mood and would like to show some love today.

For all these reasons or any others, please donate to Gmvault via Paypal or Flattr to Gmvault.
Gmvault will graciously accept any donations, but because of the Paypal and Flattr fees, may I suggest you to give at least 5 $ or 5 euros.
It is little bit more than a beer but will be tremendously helpful for developing the next Gmvault versions. Thanks a million, greatly appreciated.

Sponsoring the Gmvault development

You are a company running Gmail for Business or you are providing services around Google Apps for business.
Gmvault is more than relevant for your business and you would like to see specific features in the next versions or you would like Gmvault to be supported professionally.

You should then consider donating $100 or more to sponsor the Gmvault development. You will have the possibility to push up features in the road-map of the next Gmvault versions.
You will get access to early versions and you will be gratefully listed as a sponsor in the Gmvault Home page.
If you are interested please send me an email at guillaume.aubert(at)gmail.com.

Participate to the Gmvault development

Want to test the new versions on Windows, Mac or Linux, work on a feature, fix existing bugs or improve the code ?
